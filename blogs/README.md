## Style guide for contributors

- Cite primary sources with full titles, authors, and venues.
  Secondary commentary is fine as long as the underlying paper
  is linked.
- Include diagrams. Mermaid is the default; ASCII tables and
  preformatted charts are welcome when they render more cleanly.
- Show proofs where they fit. A 3-line derivation beats a
  paragraph of hand-waving.
- Be opinionated but stay factual. Strong claims get strong
  evidence.
- Do not use em dashes. Use commas, colons, parentheses, or
  sentence breaks.
