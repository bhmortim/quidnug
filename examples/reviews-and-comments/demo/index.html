Why this observer's rating came out where it did. Each reviewer's raw stars are multiplied by the four factors T × H × A × R. Reviewers with any factor at zero (or trust too low) are excluded entirely.

Reviewer Raw stars Weight T (trust) H (helpful) A (activity) R (recency)

Factor legend

T — Topical trust
Observer's transitive trust in the reviewer within this topic (with 0.8 decay per topic-tree hop when falling back to a parent domain).
H — Helpfulness
Ratio of HELPFUL / (HELPFUL + UNHELPFUL) votes on this reviewer from other observers the viewer trusts.
A — Activity
Log-scaled review count — raises confidence in the reviewer's pattern.
R — Recency
Exponential decay: 2-year half-life, floor 0.3 so ancient reviews never fully vanish.
